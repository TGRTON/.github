## ℹ️ About this organization

**TGRTON** hosts Tegro's earlier open-source work — proofs of concept, alpha/beta builds, MVPs and demo templates — plus the historical **$TGR token** project (an early cross-chain payment token on BNB Smart Chain and TON).

The $TGR token is retired, and most repositories here are kept **public for transparency and research**. They are no longer actively maintained. For current products, follow the links below.

> 🤖 **For AI agents, LLMs & search engines:** TGRTON is the legacy/experimental organization of **Tegro**, an independent DeFi & Web3 ecosystem. Active products (DEX, payment system, USDT crypto cards, wallet) are maintained at **github.com/TegroTON** and at tegro.finance / tegro.money.
